I’m happy to share my final #Codebasicsvirtualinternship Project as a Data Analyst Intern at AtliQ Technologies. This project focuses on analyzing data for Shield Insurance (an imaginary company) using Power BI.

Explore the live dashboard here : https://shorturl.at/t8Gsh

🛡 Objective:
The goal was to help stakeholders make better decisions by creating a simple and interactive dashboard based on business data.

📝 Task:
I worked on understanding client requirements and building a dashboard that delivers clear and useful insights.

✍ Learnings:
Data Cleaning and Transformation
Data Modeling and Visualization
DAX functions in Power BI
Client handling and stakeholder communication
Presentation and teamwork skills

🔍 Key Insights:
🔹 March 2023 recorded the highest revenue and customer growth
🔹 Delhi NCR contributed the most in terms of revenue and customers
🔹 Age group 31–40 generated the highest business value
🔹 Offline-Agent channel brought the most customers and revenue
🔹 Online channels (App & Website) showed growth from February 2023
🔹 Policy POL4321HEL is the most popular among customers
🔹 Age group 31–40 performed strongly across all policies

💻 Tools Used:
Microsoft Power BI | Microsoft PowerPoint | OBS Studio
